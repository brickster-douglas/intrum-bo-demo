The Challenge

BO Publications sends per-recipient filtered reports to external recipients via email

What BO Does Today

  • Scheduled report execution on cron
  • Per-recipient filtering via personalization rules
  • Multiple output formats (PDF, Excel, CSV)
  • Email delivery to arbitrary addresses
  • Recipient management via distribution lists

The Constraint

  • Recipients are external — not in Entra ID
  • No Databricks accounts
  • No BI tooling on recipient side
  • They expect data in their inbox
  • Tabular data: CSV, Excel, or formatted HTML

Dashboard Embedding — How It Works

External users see personalized dashboards — no Databricks account needed

Dashboard Creator (you)

One thing only — add a filter in your SQL:

SELECT * FROM portfolio_data
WHERE region = __aibi_external_value

That's it. No recipient config. No per-user setup. The dashboard is generic — it works for any value.

The external_value is NOT controlled by the end user.
It's set server-side by your application — secure by design.

Your Application (controls who sees what)

1. Anna logs into your portal
2. Your app looks up Anna → region = "Sweden"
3. Your app calls Databricks API:
    "Give me a token with external_value=Sweden"
4. Dashboard renders → Anna sees only Sweden

Next user:
Erik logs in → your app sends "Denmark"
→ Erik sees only Denmark

Same dashboard. Different data. Controlled by YOUR app.
